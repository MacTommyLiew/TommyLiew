Introduction

Welcome to the Pokemon Deck Guide! This page helps new players build and play effective Pokemon TCG decks. You'll find three complete sample decks — Fire (Charizard), Water (Blastoise), and Grass (Venusaur) — each with a full card list, strategy tips, matchup analysis, and card explanations.

Also included is an interactive Deck Builder where you can create your own deck by selecting cards. The builder tracks Pokemon, Trainer, and Energy counts and warns if you exceed four copies of a card.

Strategy Tips

Prioritize getting Charmander onto the field early. Use Professor's Research to dig for evolution pieces. Once Charizard is active, its powerful attacks can knock out most Pokemon in one or two hits. Manage Energy carefully.

Matchups

  • Strong vs Grass: Fire exploits Grass weakness.
  • Weak vs Water: Water resists Fire.
  • Even vs Psychic: Apply early pressure.

Card Explanation

Charmander – Basic starter, keep safe.

Charmeleon – Stage 1 evolution.

Charizard – Main attacker, huge damage.

Professor Research – Draw 7 cards.

Potion – Heal 30 damage.

Strategy Tips

Build your bench patiently. Use Potion to keep Squirtle/Wartortle healthy. Once Blastoise is active, deal consistent damage while your opponent struggles to break through. Use Professor Research for card advantage.

Matchups

  • Strong vs Fire: Water exploits Fire weakness.
  • Weak vs Electric: Electric threatens Water.
  • Favorable vs Fighting: Blastoise's HP outlasts them.

Card Explanation

Squirtle – Basic starter, evolve quickly.

Wartortle – Stage 1, durable.

Blastoise – Solid attacker with high HP.

Professor Research – Refresh hand.

Potion – Enhance defense.

Strategy Tips

Evolve Bulbasaur quickly. Venusaur's healing plus Potion makes it extremely durable. Don't rush; let your opponent waste resources. Use Professor Research to find pieces.

Matchups

  • Strong vs Water: Grass advantage over Water.
  • Weak vs Fire: Fire deals double damage.
  • Even vs Psychic: Healing tips the balance.

Card Explanation

Bulbasaur – Basic starter, protect it.

Ivysaur – Stage 1, moderate HP.

Venusaur – Reliable attacker with healing.

Professor Research – Draw into evolutions.

Potion – Extra recovery.
